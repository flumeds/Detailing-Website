Information/Steps included in our Standard Interior Package

Vacuuming: We use a combination of tools, including an air compressor, drill, and vacuum, to ensure a thorough removal of dirt, debris, and pet hair from your vehicle's carpets, upholstery, and other surfaces.

Wipe Down: We wipe down all surfaces of your vehicle, including dashboard, console, and door panels, to remove dust and grime and give your interior a fresh look.

Window Cleaning: We clean all interior windows, ensuring a clear and streak-free view.

Door & Trunk Jamb Cleaning: We clean the door and trunk jambs to remove dirt and debris buildup.

UV Protection Application: We apply a protective spray to your vehicle's interior surfaces, such as dashboard and door panels, to shield them from the sun's harmful UV rays, preventing fading and cracking.
