Information/Steps included in our Standard Exterior Package

Foam Bath (100% Hand Wash): We start by pre-rinsing your car to loosen dirt and grime. Then, we use a gentle foam solution to hand-wash every inch of your car's exterior, paying special attention to detail to ensure no spots are missed.

Tire & Wheel Cleaning: Our specialized tools and techniques allow us to remove tough brake dust, dirt, and grime from your wheels and tires. We use safe, professional-grade products that won't damage your tires or wheels.

Window Cleaning: We use professional-grade cleaning products and techniques to eliminate smudges and streaks from your car's windows to ensure a crystal-clear view.

Air Blowing & Towel Drying: Our Air Blowing & Towel Drying process ensures your car is dried completely, leaving no water spots or streaks behind. We use powerful air blowers to blow out all the water from hard-to-reach areas, followed by a careful towel drying to get the remaining moisture.
