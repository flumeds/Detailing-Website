Information/Steps included in our Premium Wash & Wax

Our Premium Wash & Wax Package includes everything in the Standard Foam Wash Package, as well as additional services to enhance and protect the condition of your vehicle.

Bug & Tar Remover Treatment: We apply a specialized solution to effectively remove stubborn bugs and tar from your vehicle's exterior surfaces.

Iron Removal Treatment: We use a special iron remover solution to dissolve and remove stubborn iron deposits and other contaminants from your vehicle's paint, leaving it smooth and clean

Clay Bar Treatment: We use a special clay bar to gently remove contaminants and leave your car's paint feeling smooth and clean.

Wax Application: We apply high-quality wax by hand to protect your car's paint from the elements and give it a brilliant shine that lasts up to two months.

Tire Dressing Application: We apply tire dressing to give your tires a deep, rich finish, and protect them from the damaging effects of UV rays and other environmental factors.
